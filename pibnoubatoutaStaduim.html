Ibnou batouta Staduim

Visiting Tangier? Be sure to check out the impressive Ibn Batouta Stadium, named after the famous Moroccan explorer. This multi-purpose stadium boasts a capacity of 65,000 and hosts football matches, concerts, and other major events. Completed in 2023, it's not just a sporting ground, but also a landmark of the city.
